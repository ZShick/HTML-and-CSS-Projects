My favorite characters in Apex Legends.

Gibraltar, Wattson, and Pathfinder

Gibraltar

He is a tank and is my favorite to use due to his gun shield and team utility.

Wattson

She is good for defending and holding a strategic point on the map with her electric fences.

Pathfinder

They are my favorite to play aggresive with as their grapple can get you into and out of the fight quickly.
