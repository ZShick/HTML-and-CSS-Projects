Poem

All that is gold does not glitter.
Not all those who wander are lost.
The old that is strong does not wither.
Deep roots are not reached by the frost.
From the ashes a fire shall be woken.
A light from the shadow shall spring.
Renewed shall be blade that was broken.
The crownless again shall be king.

-J. R. R. Tolkien

Bio Information

Tolkien was born on January 3, 1892, in Bloemfontein, South Africa. He was an English professor, writer, and philologist. He is most well known for writing The Hobbit and The Lord of the Rings. He effectively birthed the modern genre of Fantasy.
